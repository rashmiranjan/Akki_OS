# Idea Generator Skill

## Purpose
Generate innovative SaaS/content ideas based on market pain points and trend intelligence.

## Inputs
- Pain points (from market intelligence, Reddit, Twitter, LinkedIn)
- Trend data (spikes, viral topics)
- Competitor gaps

## Output Format
10+ ideas with:
- Problem/Pain point
- Target audience (ICP)
- Solution concept
- Unique angle (why it's different)
- Validation signals (how we know it's real)

## Idea Categories
1. Content ideas (LinkedIn posts, Twitter threads)
2. Product features/tools
3. Market plays (positioning, messaging)
4. Growth tactics

## Quality Criteria
- Specific pain point (not generic)
- Clear audience (founders, PMs, etc.)
- Actionable (can actually build/do it)
- Different from existing solutions
- Data-backed (we've seen this need)

## Execution
1. Get pain points from intelligence-db or convex
2. For each pain point, generate 2-3 angles
3. Prioritize by urgency and trend data
4. Output 10+ best ideas in JSON format
