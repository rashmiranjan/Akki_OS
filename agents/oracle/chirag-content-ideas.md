- **3-4 posts per week** (Tues, Wed, Thu, Fri)

- **1 video per week** (building trust)

### Content Mix
- 40%: Technical tutorials (AI agents, automation)
- 30%: Personal stories (vulnerability, building in public)
- 20%: Strategy/frameworks (personal branding, LinkedIn)

- **Reply to all comments within 1 hour** (algorithm boost)
- **Engage with 10 posts daily** (not just own content)

### Growth Levers
1. **Saveable content** (carousels, frameworks) → Recurrent traffic
2. **Shareable content** (open source, tools) → New followers
3. **Engagement** (comments, questions) → Algorithm boost
4. **Consistency** (3-4x/week) → Trust + recognition
