## A/B Testing Ideas for This Week

1. **Hook Test:**
   - Post A: Question hook ("Ever wondered...")
   - Post B: Statement hook ("The truth about...")
   - Measure: Engagement rate

2. **CTA Test:**
   - Post A: "Comment 'YES' below"
   - Post B: "Send me a DM"
   - Measure: Conversion rate

3. **Format Test:**
   - Post A: Text-only post
   - Post B: Post with image
   - Measure: Reach and engagement

4. **Time Test:**
   - Post A: 10 AM IST
   - Post B: 7 PM IST
   - Measure: Peak engagement time

## Metrics to Track

- **Engagement Rate:** (likes + comments + shares) / reach × 100
- **Follower Growth:** Net new followers per day
- **Click-Through Rate:** Link clicks / impressions × 100
- **Comment Quality:** Not just count, but meaningful conversations
- **Reach vs. Impressions:** Understanding unique views
